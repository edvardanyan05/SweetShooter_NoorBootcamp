# SweetShooter 🍬🚀

A vibrant, casual Android puzzle game where precision meets sweet satisfaction. Players aim and shoot colored candies from a central cannon at a dynamic, moving ring of confections traveling along a spline. Match three or more, trigger chain reactions, and clear the board!

This project was proudly developed during the **Noor Bootcamp** by **Noor Games**.

---

## 🎮 How to Play

*   **Aim:** Tap and hold anywhere on the screen to precisely aim the cannon.
*   **Shoot:** Release your touch to fire a candy toward the moving ring.
*   **Match:** Connect **3 or more candies** of the same color to destroy them.
*   **Combo:** Watch for chain reactions that trigger automatically after an explosion.
*   **Win Condition:** Clear all candies from the ring to successfully complete the level.
*   **Lose Condition:** If you run out of ammo while candies are still on the ring, it's **Game Over**.
